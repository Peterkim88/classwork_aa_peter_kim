Ingredients

  • 3 bowls steamed rice (3 cups)
  • 1 cup chopped kimchi
  • ¼ cup kimchi juice
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2-3 tablespoons gochujang
  • 3 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
  • 1 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon roasted sesame seeds
  • 1 sheet of gim, roasted and shredded

Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

This simple, simple dish is super, super tasty. It’s just a few ingredients(including kimchi), but it’s a dish much loved by Koreans.

Directions

  1. Heat up a pan. Add the vegetable oil.
  2. Add the kimchi and stir fry for 1 minute.
  3. Add rice, kimchi juice, water, and gochujang. Stir all the
    ingredients together for about 7 minutes with a wooden spoon.
  4. Add sesame oil and remove from the heat.
  5. Sprinkle with chopped green onion, roasted gim, and sesame
    seeds. Serve right away.
  6. Fry an egg and place on top with shredded seaweed as a bonus.
